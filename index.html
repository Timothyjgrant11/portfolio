Quiz App I created during the Thinkful bootcamp. The assignment was to create a 10 question quiz with a topic of your choosing. The app follows specific guidelines set up by Thinkful such as the number of questions and answer choices, feedback after every answer guess, the users current score, and the final score with the option to retake.
The Quiz App implements: -HTML -CSS -Javascript -Jquery
